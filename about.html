About Willow Print Co.

Founded with a passion for storytelling through paper, Willow Print Co. is a boutique studio specializing in custom wedding and event stationery. From intricate save-the-dates to luxurious invitations adorned with wax seals and vellum wraps, each piece is handcrafted to celebrate life’s most cherished moments.

Our mission is to blend artistry with personal connection, creating stationery that reflects your unique story. Using premium materials like 100% cotton paper, gold foil accents, and eco-friendly finishes, we ensure every design is as sustainable as it is stunning.

Based in Studio City, we work closely with clients worldwide, offering personalized consultations to bring your vision to life. Whether it’s a romantic wedding suite or a bold branding kit, we’re here to make your moments unforgettable.
