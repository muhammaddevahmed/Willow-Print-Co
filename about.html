About Willow Print Co.

Founded with a love for storytelling through paper, Willow Print Co. is a boutique stationery studio specializing in custom wedding and event paper goods. From elegant save-the-dates to thoughtfully crafted invitations, each piece is designed and handcrafted to celebrate life’s most meaningful moments.

We believe great stationery is more than just paper — it’s a lasting impression. That’s why we use premium materials like linen, cardstock, delicate vellum wraps, and modern print techniques to create timeless designs with a personal touch.

We offer one-on-one support to bring your vision to life. Whether you’re planning a wedding, announcing a graduation, or building your brand, Willow Print Co. is here to make your moments unforgettable — one detail at a time.
