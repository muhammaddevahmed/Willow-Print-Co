Wedding Stationery Checklist

Paper Options

Glossy

Vibrant and smooth with a shiny finish, perfect for bold designs and high-impact colors. Best for photo-heavy invitations or modern styles.

Matte

Smooth and non-reflective, giving a clean and elegant look. Ideal for a timeless, classic feel with easy writing capability.

Linen

A soft, textured finish that mimics the feel of fabric. Perfect for a sophisticated, upscale touch — especially popular for formal or rustic-themed weddings.

Extras Options

Custom Or Blank Vellum Wraps

Soft, translucent wraps available blank or custom-printed. A romantic way to bundle your invitation suite.

Monogrammed Wax Seal

Elegant, wax seals stamped with your initials. A timeless finishing touch.

Wax-sealed envelopes arrive pre-sealed and cannot be reopened. A few unstamped can be provided on request for quality checks.
